About Us

Luthz is a modern gastrobar in Kaunas that blends fine dining, social atmosphere, and cutting-edge AI solutions. Our mission is to create an elegant yet innovative space for young professionals and students.

Our Smart Features

  • Personalized Menu: AI-powered recommendations for dishes & drinks based on taste profiles and order history.
  • Smart Stock: Demand forecasting to optimize purchasing and cut food waste.
  • AI-Driven Promos: Suggests the best time/windows for happy hour and targeted offers.
